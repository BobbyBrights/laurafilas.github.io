about
We spend our time curating a timeless collection of key pieces, new and old, to bring you effortless looks, without the treasure hunt.
And that’s just how Pretty Odd came about. Two girls on a mission to make finding that perfect and, well, odd piece easy and most pleasant. You don’t have to worry about what happened to get your clothes and goodies here. Everything we carry is made from responsibly sourced materials and sweatshop-free. All you have to worry about is what to wear or what to decorate your side-table with.
Come into our store or shop from our online selection. We find new things all the time. Get it or regret it.
contact
Have any questions, concerns, or sugges-tions for us? Let’s get in touch.
What about returns? We gladly accept returns for new items within a period of 30 days. Just come in with your receipt (or e-receipt) and the item with tags attached. We cannot accept returns for vintage pieces. For online orders, ship the item back to us within 15 days. Shipping’s on us! Use the label included in your package.

Feel free to come in or contact us if you have any questions.
